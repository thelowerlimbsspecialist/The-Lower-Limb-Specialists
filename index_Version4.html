About Us

The lower limbs are essential for movement and stability, consisting of the hip joint, thigh, knee joint, lower leg, ankle, and foot. We provide treatment for injuries (sprains, fractures, tears), prevention (biomechanics, footwear), and long-term management.

We are HCPC registered and degree qualified. Early treatment ensures the best outcomes for mobility and comfort.
